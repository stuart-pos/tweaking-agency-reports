Dear Counsel for TrimRx:

We represent Amble Health ("Amble"), which operates the official telehealth platform at joinamble.com. Amble owns common-law and/or registered rights in the AMBLE mark and related trade dress in connection with GLP-1 weight-management telehealth services.

1. UNAUTHORIZED BRAND IMPERSONATION IN GOOGLE SEARCH RESULTS

Amble has documented a large-scale campaign in which third-party pages indexed by Google display AMBLE headlines on non-joinamble.com display URLs — particularly compromised Open Journal Systems (OJS) journal sites using pdfJsViewer open redirects — and route consumers to TrimRx landing pages, including glp-1.healthbenefit.vip. As of July 6, 2026, we have identified 155+ deceptive listings targeting the AMBLE brand. Evidence (SERP screenshots, indexed URLs, redirect chain documentation) is attached and available at our internal evidence report.

2. LIKELY AFFILIATE / PERFORMANCE-MARKETING NEXUS

The post-click destination (healthbenefit.vip / TrimRx intake) indicates that TrimRx or its authorized affiliates, agencies, or performance-marketing partners are the commercial beneficiaries of traffic obtained through deceptive use of Amble's brand. Whether or not TrimRx directly created the journal parasite pages, TrimRx is uniquely positioned to identify, terminate, and remediate affiliate partners responsible for brand bidding, impersonation, and parasitic SEO that drives traffic to TrimRx funnels.

3. LEGAL VIOLATIONS (NON-EXHAUSTIVE)

The conduct described above likely constitutes, among other things:
• Federal Lanham Act trademark infringement and false designation of origin (15 U.S.C. § 1114, § 1125(a));
• Unfair competition and consumer deception under state law;
• FTC Act § 5 unfair or deceptive acts or practices (15 U.S.C. § 45), including misleading search listings that imply affiliation with Amble Health;
• Potential contributory infringement if TrimRx knows of infringing affiliate activity and fails to act.

4. DEMANDS

On behalf of Amble Health, we demand that TrimRx, within ten (10) business days of receipt:

(a) Cease and desist all use of AMBLE and "Amble Health" and confusingly similar marks in paid and organic marketing, landing pages, affiliate creatives, comparison pages, and metadata;

(b) Direct all affiliates, lead generators, media buyers, and peptide/compounded-GLP-1 marketing partners to immediately remove AMBLE and "Amble Health" references from ads, LPs, and indexed content;

(c) Block traffic from known parasite domains (including mattioli1885journals.com pdfJsViewer paths and healthbenefit.vip subdomains) from converting on TrimRx properties, and cooperate in identifying affiliate IDs tied to those sources;

(d) Provide written confirmation of steps taken, including affiliate account terminations and takedown requests issued;

(e) Preserve all documents relating to affiliate agreements, healthbenefit.vip, Everflow/affiliate tracking IDs, and marketing assets referencing AMBLE.

5. RESERVATION OF RIGHTS

Amble Health reserves all rights and remedies, including injunctive relief, damages, attorney's fees where available, and complaints to the FTC, Google, and domain registrars. This letter is without prejudice to any claim or defense.
