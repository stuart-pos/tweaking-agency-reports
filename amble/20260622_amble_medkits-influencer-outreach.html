Pricing: Tier 1 vs micro influencers

Ben Shapiro is almost always far more expensive than a niche creator — sponsorship is priced on audience size, engagement, and network markup (Daily Wire, Blaze), not a flat per-read fee.

  • Tier 1 ballpark: $15K–$50K+ per integrated podcast read (or $25–$80+ CPM on downloads)
  • Tier 4 micro ballpark: $500–$5K per video/read, or affiliate-only
  • Volume discounts exist on multi-episode bundles but do not erase the reach gap — one Ben read can equal 10–30 micro integrations in budget
  • Niche premium: a 200K paramedic IFAK channel may charge more per view than a 2M commentary channel because buyers are purchase-ready

Recommended mix: 1–2 anchor Tier 1 deals for credibility + LP templates; parallel-test 8–12 Tier 4 creators for CPA and creative angles.

Influencer manager playbook

1. Network expansion

90-day sequence: Weeks 1–4 proof-of-concept (Deace, Buddy Brown, Layne Fable, City Prepping) → Weeks 5–8 scale (Alaska Prepper, GFG, America Out Loud, Fieldcraft) → Weeks 9–12 brand-safe outdoor (MeatEater, Expedition Overland).

2. Offer & LP design (Turley model)

  • Tier 1 podcasts: $30–45 off + free shipping (match TWC)
  • Tier 2 YouTube prep: $25–30 off or 10% affiliate
  • Tier 3 outdoor: $25 off + native gear integration
  • Tier 4 micro: 10–15% affiliate OR $25 off + product seed

LP checklist: co-branded hero + code · 2–3 hero kits · price/HSA/FSA · how-it-works · Rx disclaimers · UTMs on all CTAs.

URL pattern: joinamble.com/partners/{host}-medkits or joinamble.com/medkits/mayday-kit?ref={host}

3. Conversion strategy

  • Never send influencer traffic to GLP-1 homepage — dedicated kit LP only
  • Track intake start + purchase with partner_code
  • Disclose 1–2 week fulfillment in ad copy
  • Target LP → intake: 15–25% for prep audiences

4. Compliance

  • No prevent/treat/cure disease claims or Disease-X fear copy
  • Scenario language only: travel stomach, respiratory season, care hours away
  • Executive approval for high-controversy hosts (Candace Owens, Gateway Pundit)

5. Measurement (90-day scorecard)

CPA< 35% of kit AOV ($285–$945)

Cut rule: < 3 redemptions after 4+ reads → pause partnership.
