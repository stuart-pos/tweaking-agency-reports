A1. Selector behavior

The selector only updates the price summary inside the card. It does not swap the hero vial image or navigate away. CTA is separate.

A1b. Query-parameter defaults (deep links)

Ads / SMS can pre-select medication, plan, and (optionally) a higher starting dose via URL. Product is a bare flag (?tirz or ?sema — no value). Plan uses plan=1 or plan=3. Dose uses dose= only when the dose is above the lowest for that product (tirz lowest = 1.5mg, sema lowest = .2mg). A higher dose= flips Currently taking to Yes and selects that dose; omitting dose= (or linking the lowest dose) leaves Currently taking = No. There is no URL for “I don’t know”. Preference stays Cheapest. Invalid params fall back to the page default.

Webflow note: Mirror the same tirz/sema + plan + dose query shape so React and Webflow entry URLs stay identical. One LP template — do not rebuild a separate page per variant.

C. Shared FAQ copy (both LPs)

Heading: Common questions. Live UI is a single-open accordion; screenshots above force all answers open for content entry.

  1. How does pricing work, and what's included?
    Your price depends on the dose you start at. Once you begin, that rate stays locked in for as long as you remain a member, even if your dose increases later. That price includes your compounded GLP-1 medication, clinician review, and shipping. There are no membership fees or hidden costs.
  2. Who prescribes my medication?
    Your medication is prescribed by state-licensed U.S. clinicians and dispensed by licensed U.S. pharmacies. Mondae connects you with providers for evaluation and ongoing care through your patient portal.
  3. What dose will I be prescribed?
    Final approval, and the dosage that you are prescribed is solely at the discretion of your licensed prescriber and is based off of the information you provide during your medical intake questionnaire.
  4. When am I charged, and can I cancel anytime?
    You won't be charged until a licensed clinician approves your treatment. You can cancel your order anytime before it's dispensed or shipped by the pharmacy, with no cancellation fee. Once your order has been dispensed or shipped, it can't be canceled. To cancel before shipment, email us right away at support@mondae.com. For ongoing care, you can also cancel upcoming renewal orders at any time from your patient portal.
  5. I'm switching from another provider, can I keep my current dose?
    Yes. Tell your clinician your current medication and dose during intake, and they can continue you at that level when medically appropriate, with no need to restart from the lowest dose.
  6. What states is Mondae available in?
    Mondae is available in all 50 states.
  7. Do I need insurance?
    No, insurance is not required.
  8. Will I get charged if I don't qualify for treatment?
    No, you won't be charged until a physician approves your treatment.

Compounded medications are not FDA-approved. This handoff is a design/build brief for marketing pages, not medical or legal advice. Screenshots captured from the coded mockups on 2026-07-18.
