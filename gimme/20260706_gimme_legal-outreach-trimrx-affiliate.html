Re: Cease and Desist — Unauthorized Use of GIMME CARE Trademarks in Indexed Web Pages

Dear Sir or Madam:

We represent Gimme Care ("Gimme"), which operates the official telehealth platform at gimme.care. Gimme owns common-law and/or registered rights in the GIMME CARE mark in connection with GLP-1 weight-management telehealth services.

1. UNAUTHORIZED USE OF GIMME CARE IN GOOGLE SEARCH RESULTS

Gimme has documented multiple pages on your domain(s) indexed by Google that display GIMME CARE in titles, snippets, or page copy while the display URL is not gimme.care. Examples include paths on dshafjk.com and taoaadt.com that use "Gimme Care" in headlines for semaglutide and tirzepatide review content. As of July 6, 2026, we have identified 22+ such indexed URLs. Gimme did not authorize this use. Evidence (SERP screenshots, indexed URLs) is attached.

2. CONSUMER DECEPTION

These pages are designed to capture consumers searching for Gimme Care GLP-1 services. They create a false impression that Gimme Care endorses, operates, or is affiliated with your site. Gimme Care is not affiliated with your domain(s).

3. LEGAL VIOLATIONS (NON-EXHAUSTIVE)

The conduct described above likely constitutes, among other things:
• Federal Lanham Act trademark infringement and false designation of origin (15 U.S.C. § 1114, § 1125(a));
• Unfair competition and consumer deception under state law;
• FTC Act § 5 unfair or deceptive acts or practices (15 U.S.C. § 45).

4. DEMANDS

On behalf of Gimme Care, we demand that you, within ten (10) business days of receipt:

(a) Remove or disable all pages on your domain(s) that use GIMME CARE, "Gimme Care", or confusingly similar marks in titles, metadata, body copy, or URL slugs;

(b) Implement controls to prevent republication of Gimme Care-branded SEO pages;

(c) Provide written confirmation of takedown, including a list of URLs removed;

(d) Preserve logs, affiliate agreements, and traffic-source records for pages referencing Gimme Care since June 1, 2026.

5. RESERVATION OF RIGHTS

Gimme Care reserves all rights and remedies, including injunctive relief, damages, attorney's fees where available, and complaints to Google, registrars, and the FTC. This letter is without prejudice to any claim or defense.
