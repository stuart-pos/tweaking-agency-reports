Executive summary

AltRx is the newest aggressive entrant in Gimme's NB + Shopping auction — climbing from near-zero to ~25% impression share by July 6 while Gimme fell from a ~30% mid-April peak to ~10%. AltRx now outranks Gimme 2.5:1 in the same filtered campaigns.

Google AI Overviews cite negative Reddit sentiment for both brands, but AltRx's late-June surge happened despite an FDA warning letter (Jun 8). Verdict: Single-account price aggressor Recovery path: restore Mar/Apr LP-first economics before chasing rank.
