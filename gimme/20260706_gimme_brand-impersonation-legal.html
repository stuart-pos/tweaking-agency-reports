These appear to be affiliate SEO / review-farm pages rather than compromised journals.

TrimRx and healthbenefit.vip are not implicated in the Gimme findings. We have no verified redirect chain from Gimme Care affiliate-farm URLs to glp-1.healthbenefit.vip or TrimRx. That parasite-journal → healthbenefit.vip pattern is documented on Amble only. Gimme counsel should pursue the third-party domain operators and registrars behind the spam pages — not TrimRx — unless future evidence links a specific destination.

Google paid ads — methodology (parasite display-URL model)

Deceptive listings in this campaign do not show TrimRx or healthbenefit.vip in Google. They show a parasite display URL on a compromised journal domain (e.g. mattioli1885journals.com/.../pdfJsViewer). The affiliate destination (glp-1.healthbenefit.vip) appears only after click-through redirects.

What we checked (2026-07-06)

  1. Live SERP paid-slot scan — DataForSEO serp/google/organic/live/advanced, US (2840), desktop + mobile, depth 50. For each brand query, flag items where type is paid/ad/shopping or ad_aclk_url is present, headline mentions the brand, and display URL matches parasite patterns (pdfJsViewer, signOut?source, journal hosts, .top redirectors).
  2. Not used as primary test: Ads Transparency pulls on trimrx.com / healthbenefit.vip — those domains are post-click destinations, not what Google displays in the listing.

Result: 0 paid listings with parasite display URLs + brand in headline for both Amble and Gimme Care on this scan date.

Recommended ongoing monitor (weekly)

  1. SERP parasite watch — Re-run scan-brand-parasite-serp.js (or schedule via cron). Alert when paid_parasite count > 0 or organic_parasite_unique_urls increases week-over-week.
  2. Brand query set — Maintain 15–20 queries per brand: brand name + GLP-1 terms, site:mattioli1885journals.com {brand}, pdfJsViewer {brand}, semaglutide/tirzepatide variants.
  3. Parasite URL signatures — Match display/landing URLs against:
    • /plugins/generic/pdfJsViewer/
    • signOut?source=. or signOut%3Fsource%3D.
    • Journal hosts: mattioli1885journals.com, journals.econsciences.com, jmbfs.org
    • Attacker TLDs: ojsnua44.top, dmouw13.top, cami680.top
  4. Paid vs organic — Treat any SERP item with type: paid or ad_aclk_url + parasite display URL as P0 legal alert (higher severity than organic parasite SEO).
  5. Manual spot-check — Weekly screenshot of top 3 brand queries in a clean browser profile; compare Sponsored label vs organic journal-domain results.
  6. Google Ads Transparency (secondary) — Search advertiser name contains "Amble" or "Gimme" and inspect creative preview images for journal-domain display URLs (OCR on preview if metadata lacks headline text).
  7. Post-click verification — For any new parasite URL, sandboxed click trace: indexed URL → attacker subdomain → final LP domain. Log final URL separately from display URL.
