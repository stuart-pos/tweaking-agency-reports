Research criteria learnings

  1. Verify employment — confirm journalist still at outlet before Top 5.
  2. Match podcast beat to story — bootstrap keyword ≠ fit if software-only or DEI-focused.
  3. Healthcare founders — exclude GLP-1-critical and big-pharma trade reporters.
  4. Local geography — county reporters ≠ statewide program stories.
  5. Podcast audience floor — flag sub-scale shows when founder time is limited.
  6. Paid-to-play — sponsored strategy only (Grit Daily).
  7. Pitched Previously — follow-up lane; do not consume net-new Top 5 slots.

Media list expansion strategy

  1. Second-chance hiring / workforce DEI — HR Executive, SHRM, WorkingNation.
  2. Faith-driven business leadership — Christianity Today Business, Relevant Podcast, Praxis Journal.
  3. DTC telehealth / GLP-1 access — KFF Health News, Modern Healthcare. Avoid STAT and Fierce Healthcare.
